    Enter in the games Apex

    You are in a 3 man squad team fighting against 19 others (i.e. 60 players total on the map)
    Your goal ? Becoming the Apex's Champion, the last man standing.

    You can pick up many different types of weapons :
    - Assault rifle
    - Machine guns
    - Long distance
    - Snipers
    - Shot gun
    - Hand gun

    They are many maps available on Apex, per season you'll have the opportunity to play at least on 3 different maps.
    The said maps are as follows :
    - King's Canyon
    - World's Edge
    - Olympus
    - Storm Point
    - Broken Moon

    Ranked Queue

    The well known ranked mod that's present on many other battle royale games, is of course present there too.
    In this mod, you fight against players that's match your level and always in trios.
    The more you get kills, the more you earn points and the more your rank up !
    But be aware, ranking is important. Without any good top (top 1/2/3), your kills alone will not make you win points !
    In this queue, your skills and your braincells will be required.

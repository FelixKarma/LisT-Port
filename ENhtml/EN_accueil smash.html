    • What's Super Smash Bros ?

    Super smash bros is a platforming / versus fighting game published by Nintendo and developped by HAL Laboratory, Sora ltd and Bandai Namico Games. In this game you take part in fights featuring many characters from the Nitendo univers (and many guests from other games such as Kazuya Mishima from the Tekken franchise).
    The game's goal is simply to eject out of the arena your oponnent(s). There are no regular life bars, but instead a damage counter, the more the percentage of damages taken is high the greater chances you got to be taken down.
